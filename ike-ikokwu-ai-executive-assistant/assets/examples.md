### Executive Summary

David, thanks for the great conversation last Tuesday. You mentioned that Greenleaf Landscaping has been growing through word-of-mouth for eight years, but you're ready to start showing up when people search for landscaping services in the Westlake area. Right now, your online presence isn't reflecting the quality of work your team does — and that means potential customers are finding your competitors first.

Here's what I propose: a complete digital marketing foundation that gets your website, Google Business Profile, and content strategy working together. Within four weeks, you'll have a professional website that ranks for local searches, an optimized GBP listing that attracts clicks, and a 3-month content plan to keep the momentum going.

The goal is simple: when someone in Westlake searches for landscaping, Greenleaf shows up — and looks like the obvious choice.

### Scope of Work

**PHASE 1: Discovery & Strategy (Week 1)**
- Audit current online presence (website, GBP, social, reviews)
- Research local competitors and keyword opportunities
- Define target services and service area for SEO focus
- Develop content strategy and 3-month editorial calendar
- *Milestone: Strategy document delivered for your review*

**PHASE 2: Website Build (Weeks 2-3)**
- Design and build a professional single-page website
- Write copy for all sections: hero, services, about, testimonials, contact
- Optimize for local SEO: "landscaping in Westlake" and related terms
- Mobile-responsive, fast-loading, professional photography placement
- *Milestone: Website ready for your review*

**PHASE 3: GBP & Launch (Week 4)**
- Optimize Google Business Profile: categories, description, services, photos
- Set up review request system (email template for asking customers)
- Write first month of GBP posts (4 posts)
- Launch website and verify Google indexing
- *Milestone: Everything live and running*

**NOT INCLUDED:**
- Google Ads or paid advertising (available as a separate engagement)
- Ongoing social media management
- Photography (I'll use your existing photos or stock)
- Ongoing monthly content creation (available as a retainer add-on)

## Example 2: Web Developer → SaaS Startup

**Proposal Brief:**
```
Client:       Alex Rivera, CTO at DataPipe (Series A startup)
Industry:     SaaS / Technology
Project:      Dashboard redesign — new data visualization features
Style:        Minimal
Pricing:      Good/Better/Best, target $12,000
Timeline:     6 weeks
```

### Cover Page
```
DASHBOARD REDESIGN
DataPipe

For: Alex Rivera, CTO
From: Jordan Park, Park Development
March 28, 2026 | Valid 30 days
```

### Executive Summary

Alex — per our call Thursday, DataPipe's current dashboard is functional but isn't keeping up with what your enterprise customers expect. The main pain points: users can't customize their views, the data visualizations are static, and the export functionality is limited. Your team is getting support tickets about these issues weekly, and your sales team is losing enterprise deals where competitors have stronger reporting UIs.

I'll redesign the dashboard front-end with dynamic data visualizations, customizable widgets, and a modern export system. Your existing API stays untouched — this is purely a front-end overhaul that connects to your current data layer.

End state: a dashboard your sales team uses as a selling point instead of an objection to overcome.

### Scope

**Phase 1: Audit + Design (Week 1-2)**
- Audit current dashboard: component structure, data flow, pain points
- Design new UI: wireframes for 3 key views (overview, detail, custom)
- Present designs for approval

**Phase 2: Build (Week 3-5)**
- Implement new visualization components
- Build customizable widget system (drag/drop layout)
- Implement export functionality (PDF, CSV, PNG)
- Connect all components to existing API endpoints

**Phase 3: QA + Deploy (Week 6)**
- Cross-browser testing
- Performance optimization
- Staging deployment + team review
- Production deployment with rollback plan

**Not included:** Backend/API changes, mobile app, new data pipelines, user authentication changes.

### Deliverables

☐ Redesigned dashboard front-end (production-ready code)

☐ Export module (PDF, CSV, PNG)
