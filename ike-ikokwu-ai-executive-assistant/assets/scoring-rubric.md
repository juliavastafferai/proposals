# Proposal Scoring Rubric

Score every proposal before showing it to the user. Minimum: 80/100.

---

## Proposal Scoring (100 points)

| Category | Points | What to Evaluate |
|----------|--------|------------------|
| Cover Page | /5 | Client name correct? Project title clear? Date and validity period? Professional appearance? |
| Executive Summary | /15 | References the meeting/conversation? Shows understanding of their problem? Clear approach? Expected outcome stated? |
| Scope of Work | /15 | Specific and measurable tasks? No ambiguity? Out-of-scope section included? Phased logically? |
| Deliverables | /10 | Itemized list? Each deliverable is a noun (thing received)? Format specified? |
| Timeline | /10 | Realistic with buffer? Milestones defined? Client dependencies noted? |
| Pricing | /15 | Good/better/best tiers (if applicable)? Recommended option marked? Payment terms clear? Anchored against value? |
| Social Proof | /5 | Relevant testimonials or credentials? Specific results? If placeholders, well-positioned? |
| Professionalism | /10 | Clean formatting? Consistent style? No typos? Appropriate tone for the industry? |
| Persuasion | /5 | Benefits over features? More "you" than "we"? Creates motivation? |
| Terms & Next Steps | /5 | Terms clear and fair? Next steps dead simple? Contact info included? |
| Client Fit | /5 | Tone matches industry? Language mirrors how they talk? Feels custom, not templated? |

### Scoring Guide (per category)

| Score Range | Level | Meaning |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| 90-100% of category max | Exceptional | Would impress any client |
| 70-89% of category max | Strong | Solid, minor polish possible |
| 50-69% of category max | Adequate | Specific issues to fix |
| Below 50% of category max | Weak | Significant revision needed |

Example: Executive Summary (/15) — Exceptional = 14-15, Strong = 11-13, Adequate = 8-10, Weak = 0-7

## Type-Specific Scoring Adjustments

> **AGENT: For non-service proposals, adapt the category definitions:**

### Partnership Proposals
| Category | Evaluate Instead |
|----------|-----------------|
| Scope of Work (/15) | **Partnership Structure** — Mutual roles defined? Contributions clear? Not one-sided? |
| Deliverables (/10) | **Mutual Benefits** — Shared outcomes listed? Tangible for both parties? |
| Timeline (/10) | **Duration & Terms** — Partnership length? Renewal terms? Exit terms? |
| Pricing (/15) | **Partnership Terms** — Revenue share clear? Resource commitments defined? |

### Membership Proposals
| Category | Evaluate Instead |
|----------|-----------------|
| Scope of Work (/15) | **Membership Benefits** — What members get? Clearly differentiated tiers? |
| Deliverables (/10) | **Access & Resources** — Community, content, coaching described specifically? |
| Timeline (/10) | **Enrollment & Commitment** — Billing cycle? Cancellation terms? |
| Pricing (/15) | **Tier Pricing** — Tiers clearly differentiated? Value clear at each level? |

### Sponsorship Proposals
| Category | Evaluate Instead |
|----------|-----------------|
| Scope of Work (/15) | **Opportunity Overview** — Event/property clearly described? Audience data specific? |
| Deliverables (/10) | **Sponsor Benefits** — Deliverables per level? Logo, booth, speaking, mentions? |
| Timeline (/10) | **Event Details** — Dates, logistics, materials deadlines? |
| Pricing (/15) | **Sponsorship Levels** — Tiers differentiated? ROI/value implied? |

### Speaking Proposals
| Category | Evaluate Instead |
|----------|-----------------|
| Scope of Work (/15) | **Speaker Profile & Topics** — Topics relevant? Format options clear? |
| Deliverables (/10) | **What the Event Gets** — Keynote, materials, handouts described? |
| Timeline (/10) | **Logistics** — AV needs, travel, rehearsal, recording rights? |
| Pricing (/15) | **Speaker Fee** — Fee + expenses separated? Payment terms clear? |
