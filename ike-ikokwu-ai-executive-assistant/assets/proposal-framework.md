# Proposal Framework

The complete structure for every proposal. Follow this order — it's designed to build toward the "yes."

---

## The Psychology of Proposal Order

```
1. Cover         → First impression, professionalism
2. Summary       → "We understand your problem"
3. Scope         → "Here's exactly what we'll do"
4. Deliverables  → "Here's what you'll receive"
5. Timeline      → "Here's when it happens"
6. Investment    → "Here's what it costs" (AFTER they see the value)
7. About         → "Here's why we're the right choice"
8. Social Proof  → "Others trust us too"
9. Terms         → "Here's how it works"
10. Next Steps   → "Here's what to do now"
```

**Key:** Pricing comes AFTER scope, deliverables, and timeline. By the time they see the number, they already understand the full value.

---

## Section-by-Section Guide

### 1. Cover Page

```
{{PROJECT TITLE}}

Prepared for: {{client_name}}, {{company}}
Prepared by: {{your_name}}, {{your_company}}
Date: {{date}}
Valid until: {{expiry_date — default 14 days, configurable via MEMORY.md}}
```

Keep it clean. No long descriptions on the cover.

### 2. Executive Summary (150-250 words)

**Purpose:** Show the client you understand their problem and have a clear plan.

**Structure:**
```
Paragraph 1 — The Situation:
"{{Client_name}}, based on our conversation on {{date}}, you're looking to
{{what they need}}. Currently, {{the problem or challenge they described}}."

Paragraph 2 — The Approach:
"We propose {{your solution in 2-3 sentences}}. This approach will
{{primary benefit}} while {{secondary benefit}}."

Paragraph 3 — The Expected Outcome:
"By the end of this engagement, you'll have {{specific deliverables}}
that {{outcome/result they care about}}."
```

**Rules:**
- Reference the conversation or meeting (shows you listened)
- Use THEIR words for the problem (mirror their language)
- No jargon unless the client used it first
- This section sells — the rest of the proposal details

### 3. Scope of Work

**Purpose:** Exactly what you'll do. No ambiguity.

**Format by phase:**
```
PHASE 1: {{Phase Name}} ({{Duration}})
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- {{Task 1}}: {{Brief description}}
- {{Task 2}}: {{Brief description}}
- {{Task 3}}: {{Brief description}}
Milestone: {{What's complete at end of this phase}}

PHASE 2: {{Phase Name}} ({{Duration}})
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
...
```

**Rules:**
- Be specific: "Design 3 homepage concepts" not "design work"
- Include what's IN scope AND what's NOT in scope
- "Out of scope" prevents scope creep later — don't skip it

**Out of Scope section:**
```
NOT INCLUDED IN THIS PROPOSAL:
- {{Item 1}} (available as an add-on)
- {{Item 2}} (recommend {{alternative}})
- {{Item 3}}
```

### 4. Deliverables

**Purpose:** What the client actually receives. Tangible items.

**Format:**
```
DELIVERABLES
━━━━━━━━━━━━
☐ {{Deliverable 1}} — {{Format: PDF, HTML, Figma, etc.}}
☐ {{Deliverable 2}} — {{Format}}
☐ {{Deliverable 3}} — {{Format}}
☐ {{Deliverable 4}} — {{Format}}
```

**Rules:**
- Every deliverable is a noun (a thing they receive), not a verb (something you do)
- Include the format/medium
- Number them for easy reference in discussions

### 5. Timeline

**Format:**
```
TIMELINE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Phase 1: {{name}}     {{start}} → {{end}}
  ⬡ Milestone: {{deliverable or checkpoint}}

Phase 2: {{name}}     {{start}} → {{end}}
  ⬡ Milestone: {{deliverable or checkpoint}}

Phase 3: {{name}}     {{start}} → {{end}}
  ⬡ Milestone: {{deliverable or checkpoint}}

TOTAL DURATION: {{X weeks/months}}
PROJECT COMPLETE: {{target_date}}
```

**Rules:**
- Include buffer (things always take longer)
- Mention client dependencies: "Timeline assumes client feedback within 3 business days at each milestone"
- Be honest about realistic timelines — overpromising destroys trust

### 6. Investment (Pricing)

See `assets/pricing-options.md` for full guide.

**Default: Good/Better/Best format.**

**Single price format (if user prefers):**
```
INVESTMENT
━━━━━━━━━━
Total: ${{price}}

Payment Schedule:
- {{amount}} due upon signing ({{percentage}}%)
- {{amount}} due at {{milestone}} ({{percentage}}%)
- {{amount}} due upon completion ({{percentage}}%)
```

### 7. About You / Credentials

**150-200 words.** Brief, relevant, credibility-focused.

```
ABOUT {{YOUR_NAME/COMPANY}}
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

{{2-3 sentences: who you are, what you do, how long you've been doing it}}

{{1-2 sentences: relevant experience for THIS project specifically}}

{{Credentials: certifications, awards, notable clients (with permission)}}

**Optional — Team Section (recommended for service businesses):**
If you have a team photo (e.g., `assets/vastaffer-team-group.jpg`), add this block:

```
OUR TEAM
━━━━━━━━━━
![Our Team](assets/vastaffer-team-group.jpg)

{{Brief description — e.g., "We're a tight-knit team of AI-augmented specialists supporting entrepreneurs across finance, marketing, and operations."}}
```
```

### 8. Social Proof

See `assets/testimonial-blocks.md` for templates.

**Include 1-2 testimonials** most relevant to this project type. If none available, use placeholders.

**Note:** If you added a Team section above, Social Proof can be combined or kept separate. Use whichever order flows naturally for your client.

### 9. Terms & Conditions

See `assets/terms-templates.md` for standard sections.

Keep it clear and fair. Don't bury scary clauses — transparency builds trust.

### 10. Next Steps

**Purpose:** Make it dead simple to say yes.

**By proposal type:**

*Service / Sales:*
```
NEXT STEPS
━━━━━━━━━━
1. Review this proposal
2. Reply with your selected option (A, B, or C)
3. We'll send an invoice for the initial payment
4. Upon payment, we begin Phase 1 on {{date}}

Questions? Reply to this email or call {{phone}}.
```

*Partnership:*
```
NEXT STEPS
━━━━━━━━━━
1. Review this proposal
2. Reply to confirm interest
3. We'll schedule a follow-up call to finalize terms
4. Sign the partnership agreement and begin {{first action}}

Questions? Reply to this email or call {{phone}}.
```

*Membership:*
```
NEXT STEPS
━━━━━━━━━━
1. Review the membership options above
2. Click the link below to enroll (or reply with your questions)
3. You'll get immediate access to {{key benefit}}
4. Welcome email with onboarding details follows within 24 hours

Questions? Reply to this email or call {{phone}}.
```

*Sponsorship:*
```
NEXT STEPS
━━━━━━━━━━
1. Review the sponsorship packages above
2. Reply to confirm your preferred level
3. We'll send the sponsorship agreement within 24 hours
4. Upon signing, your placement begins on {{date}}

Questions? Reply to this email or call {{phone}}.
```

*Speaking:*
```
NEXT STEPS
━━━━━━━━━━
1. Confirm the date and preferred topic
2. We'll send the speaker agreement and logistics form
3. Upon signing + deposit, the date is locked in
4. Pre-event call scheduled {{X}} weeks before the event

Questions? Reply to this email or call {{phone}}.
```

**Rules:**
- Number the steps
- Make step 1 easy ("reply to this email")
- Include a timeline: "If we start by {{date}}, we can deliver by {{date}}"
- Always include contact info for questions

## Type-Specific Section Replacements

> **AGENT: For non-service proposals, replace certain sections with the alternatives below.**

### Partnership Proposals

Replace **Scope of Work** with:
```
PARTNERSHIP OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
{{Strategic rationale — why this partnership makes sense for both parties}}

YOUR ROLE:
- {{What you contribute — expertise, audience, platform, resources}}

THEIR ROLE:
- {{What they contribute}}

MUTUAL BENEFITS:
- {{Shared outcome 1}}
- {{Shared outcome 2}}
- {{Shared outcome 3}}
```

Replace **Investment** with:
```
PARTNERSHIP TERMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Duration:    {{X months/years, with renewal option}}
Exclusivity: {{Exclusive in category / Non-exclusive}}
Revenue:     {{Revenue share model, if applicable}}
Resources:   {{What each party invests — time, money, tools}}
```

### Membership Proposals

Replace **Scope of Work** with:
```
MEMBERSHIP OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
{{What the membership provides and who it's for}}

WHAT'S INCLUDED:
- {{Benefit 1 — community, content, coaching, etc.}}
- {{Benefit 2}}
- {{Benefit 3}}

COMMITMENT:
- {{Monthly / Annual billing}}
- {{What's expected of members}}
```

Replace **Investment** with tier structure (use pricing-options.md but frame as access levels, not project scope).

### Sponsorship Proposals

Replace **Scope of Work** with:
```
OPPORTUNITY OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
{{Event/property description}}

AUDIENCE:
- Size: {{number}}
- Demographics: {{description}}
- Engagement: {{metrics — attendance, views, click rates}}
```

Replace **Investment** with:
```
SPONSORSHIP LEVELS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🥉 Bronze — ${{price}}
   {{Logo placement, mention in newsletter}}

🥈 Silver — ${{price}}
   Everything in Bronze, plus:
   {{Booth space, social mention, email feature}}

🥇 Gold — ${{price}}
   Everything in Silver, plus:
   {{Speaking slot, banner placement, exclusive category}}

👑 Title Sponsor — ${{price}}
   Everything in Gold, plus:
   {{Name in event title, keynote intro, VIP access}}
```

### Speaking Proposals

Replace **Scope of Work** with:
```
SPEAKER PROFILE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
{{Brief speaker bio and expertise areas}}

AVAILABLE TOPICS:
1. {{Topic 1}} — {{one-line description}}
2. {{Topic 2}} — {{one-line description}}
3. {{Topic 3}} — {{one-line description}}

FORMAT OPTIONS:
- Keynote (45-60 min)
- Workshop (half-day or full-day)
- Panel participation
- Fireside chat
```

Replace **Investment** with:
```
SPEAKER FEE
━━━━━━━━━━━
Keynote:   ${{fee}}
Workshop:  ${{fee}}

LOGISTICS REQUIREMENTS:
- {{AV needs — projector, clicker, lavalier mic}}

```
