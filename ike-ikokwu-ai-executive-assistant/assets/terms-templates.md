# Terms & Conditions Templates

Standard terms sections for proposals. Select and customize based on the project type. Keep it clear, fair, and professional.

---

## Core Terms (Include in Every Proposal)

### Payment Terms
```
PAYMENT
- Invoices are due within {{X}} business days of receipt
- Late payments incur a {{X}}% monthly fee after {{X}} days
- Project work pauses if payment is more than {{X}} days overdue
- Accepted methods: {{bank transfer, credit card, PayPal, etc.}}
```

### Revisions
```
REVISIONS
- This proposal includes {{X}} rounds of revisions per deliverable
- A "revision" is a set of changes submitted at one time
- Changes to the project scope (new features, new deliverables) are
  not revisions — they require a scope change agreement
- Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are billed at
  ${{rate}}/hour
```

### Timeline
```
TIMELINE
- The timeline outlined in this proposal assumes:
  - Project begins within {{X}} days of signed agreement
  - Client provides feedback within {{X}} business days at each milestone
  - All required materials (copy, images, credentials) are provided by {{date}}
- Delays in client feedback or materials will extend the timeline accordingly
```

### Cancellation
```
CANCELLATION
- Either party may cancel the project with {{X}} days written notice
- Upon cancellation, the client pays for all work completed to date
- Deposits are {{refundable up to X days / non-refundable}}
- Work in progress will be delivered as-is upon cancellation
```

## Intellectual Property

### Standard (Most Common)
```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
- Upon full payment, all deliverables become the property of the client
- The provider retains the right to display the work in their portfolio
  (unless a confidentiality agreement is in place)
- Source files and working documents are included in the deliverables
  unless otherwise specified
```

### With Portfolio Rights
```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
- Upon full payment, all deliverables become the property of the client
- The provider retains the right to:
  - Display the work in their portfolio and case studies
  - Reference the project in marketing materials
  - Use anonymized results in testimonials
- The client may request removal of portfolio references in writing
```

### Confidential / NDA
```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & CONFIDENTIALITY
- Upon full payment, all deliverables become the property of the client
- The provider will not display, reference, or disclose the work
  without prior written consent from the client
- Both parties agree to keep project details confidential
```

## Scope Changes

```
SCOPE CHANGES
- This proposal covers the scope described in the "Scope of Work" section
- Any additions, changes, or expansions to the scope require:
  1. Written request from the client
  2. A scope change estimate from the provider
  3. Written approval before work begins
- Scope changes may affect timeline and pricing
- Minor adjustments within the spirit of the original scope are handled
  at the provider's discretion
```

## Warranty / Guarantee

### Satisfaction Guarantee (for service businesses)
```
SATISFACTION GUARANTEE
- If you are not satisfied with the deliverables, we will revise them
  at no additional cost within {{X}} days of delivery
- This guarantee covers quality of work, not changes in project direction
```

### Results Disclaimer
```
RESULTS DISCLAIMER
- While we will deliver the work described in this proposal to our
  highest professional standard, we cannot guarantee specific business
  results (revenue, leads, traffic, etc.) as outcomes depend on many
  factors outside our control
```

## Selection Guide

> **AGENT: Include these terms based on proposal type:**
>
> | Proposal Type | Include |
> |-------------|---------|
> | Service (all) | Payment, Revisions, Timeline, Cancellation, Validity |
> | Service + creative/design | + IP (standard or portfolio), + Scope Changes |
> | Service + consulting/strategy | + Confidential IP, + Results Disclaimer |
> | Service + ongoing retainer | + Communication, + Scope Changes |
> | Service + high-ticket ($5K+) | + Warranty, + Scope Changes, + Communication |
> | Partnership | Partnership Terms, Cancellation, Communication, Confidentiality, Validity |
> | Membership | Membership Terms, Validity |
> | Sponsorship | Sponsorship Terms, Validity |
> | Speaking | Speaking Terms, Validity |
>
> Keep terms proportional to the deal size. A $500 project doesn't need two pages of legal language.
> Partnership and sponsorship proposals should always include confidentiality terms.
