Executive Summary

You're building something meaningful — a nonprofit focused on spiritual development that's still in its early fundraising stages. Right now you need reliable, skilled support to finish and maintain your WordPress website, set up donation integrations, and eventually automate your donor communications. You don't need a full-time hire. You need a flexible, project-based team that can handle web development, integrations, CRM, and creative tasks as they come up.

That's exactly what VA Staffer's Specialized VA Plan delivers — dedicated specialist hours across web development, CRM automation, integrations, and creative services, billed only for the time you use, with hours that roll over if you don't use them.

Scope of Work

Phase 1: Website Completion & Donation Setup (Weeks 1-3)

  • Finish and maintain your WordPress website — pages, links, blog, media
  • Add donation buttons/links (Stripe, Zeffy, PayPal, GoFundMe)
  • Improve the donor journey — from landing page to thank-you confirmation
  • Set up secure access sharing via LastPass so you control who has access, when

Phase 2: Integrations & Automation (Weeks 3-5)

  • Connect donation platforms (Stripe/Zeffy, PayPal, GoFundMe) to your CRM via Zapier, Make, or N8N
  • Set up automated thank-you messages triggered by donations
  • Explore donor tracking, tagging, and notes in your CRM
  • Test end-to-end: donor gives → CRM updated → thank-you sent

Phase 3: Ongoing Support & Growth (Ongoing)

  • Regular website updates — new pages, blog posts, media uploads
  • Fundraising email content creation for donation campaigns
  • Video editing for website, marketing, and fundraising use
  • AI Specialist tasks as needed (future option — see below)
