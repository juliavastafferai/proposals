Executive Summary

You're building something that matters — a nonprofit focused on spiritual development, still in its early fundraising stages. You need reliable, skilled support to finish your WordPress website, connect your donation platforms, and eventually automate your donor communications. You don't need a full-time hire — you need a flexible, project-based team.

VA Staffer's Specialized VA Plan gives you exactly that: dedicated specialist hours across web development, CRM automation, integrations, and creative services — billed only for time used, with hours that roll over when you don't.

Scope of Work

Phase 1 — Website Completion & Donation Setup (Weeks 1–3)

  • Finish and maintain your WordPress website — pages, links, blog, media
  • Add donation buttons and links for Stripe, Zeffy, PayPal, and GoFundMe
  • Improve the donor journey from landing page to thank-you confirmation
  • Set up secure access sharing via LastPass — you control who has access, when

Phase 2 — Integrations & Automation (Weeks 3–5)

  • Connect donation platforms to your CRM via Zapier, Make, or N8N
  • Set up automated thank-you messages triggered by each donation
  • Explore donor tracking, tagging, and notes in your CRM
  • Test end-to-end: donor gives → CRM updated → thank-you sent

Phase 3 — Ongoing Support & Growth (Ongoing)

  • Regular website updates — new pages, blog posts, media uploads
  • Fundraising email content for donation campaigns
  • Video editing for website, marketing, and fundraising
  • AI Specialist tasks as needed (see Option C below)
